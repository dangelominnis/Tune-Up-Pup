About Us

We'll Keep You & Your Pup Happy

When I was 12 years old, my grandfather asked me “if you could, what type of business would you start”. Little did I know that question would put me on a path to establish a passion and my future business.

My grandfather helped me purchase my very own AKC registered Chocolate Labs. I then learned how to breed and train dogs. My fascination to better understand how dogs think, and how they communicate, drove me to learn all that I could at a young age.

There has been countless hours of research, hands on experiences with thousands of dogs, and training from Cesar Millan “AKA - the Dog Whisperer”. What started as a hobby helping out my friends and family, has now turned into a passion to help dog owners understand to live in the moment with their dogs, and bring back the joy of “WHY” they brought a dog into their life.

How Training Works

Service 1

Rehabilitation and training based on you and your families needs. And a education of the way the “pack” operates and the way a dog thinks. Mastering the walk is critical and ultimately the decision maker in your packs success.

Service 2

Prestige Puppy is our premium puppy training service and package. Your puppy will stay with the trainer for 2-4 weeks learning all basic commands and socializing skills. Your puppy will be delivered with complete care package and everything needed for proper care and training, and will also come with consultation and training sessions.

Service 3

E-collar training and guidance. Teaching your K9 a new language. Just as they understand the language of the leash, they will understand the language of the e-collar. Perfect for outdoor activities. Includes collar and proper use and training. (Not used as a focused correctional method)

Step 1

Tune-Up Pups heart and mission is to bring back the “why” and “joy” of having a K9 in your life. Rehabilitation and training are a must in dog ownership. Understanding and learning the how dogs think differently from humans will completely change your prospective and approach to training your dog.

Step 2

We understand that bringing a new puppy into your life can be challenging. So what if you could bring a trained puppy into you family? One that will have basic obedience, crate trained, house broken, leash trained, socialized…. and so much more! Puppies will live with trainers for 2-4 weeks and be socialized and will be brought into many different environments and experiences. In the end you will have a well balanced puppy who knows their role in the pack.

Step 3

The e-collar is a very controversial subject. Is it humane, is safe, do dogs understand what’s happening? The answer is YES to all of these questions. If properly used, and properly trained. Is this mandatory…? Absolutely not! But it can be a great tool to have and use. Just as you have learned that when you phone vibrates it means you are receiving a text or call, in the same way we can use a e-collar to get the attention of our K9’s. This is a advanced training and should be taught once you K9 has all basic training completed, walks properly on the leash, and knows their place in the pack.

Are you wanting to get a puppy but might not want to or know how to start off a puppy in its early stages of life? If this sounds like you or if you know someone in this situation, then Prestige Puppy is your answer!
